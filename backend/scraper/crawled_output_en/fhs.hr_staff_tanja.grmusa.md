#### Biography
She has finished Communication Studies and Croatology at Croatian Studies, University of Zagreb. She received her PhD on postgraduate doctoral study programme at Department of information and communication sciences at Faculty of humanities and social sciences University of Zagreb. She has been participated in numerous scientific and professional conferences in Croatia and has published several scientific and professional articles in different journals. On Faculty for Croatian Studies she teaches Media management on graduate study programme at Department of Communication Studies. Her research interest are mainly related to communication studies, intercultural communication, business communication, media management, journalistic practice and mass media impact.
