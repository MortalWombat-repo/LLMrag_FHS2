**Description:**  
> Selected topics from the Croatian history  
>    
>  1. Introduction  
>  2. Prehistory  
>  3. Classical Age  
>  4. Field class - Archaeological museum in Zagreb  
>  5. Croatia in Early Middle Age - where did the Croats came and when (and why) ?  
>  6. Kingdom of Croatia - history and art  
>  7. Relations with Hungary and Austria  
>  8. Venetians and the Croatian coast  
>  9. Culturally and scientific developments and most prominent people of the XVIth-XIXth century period  
>  10. Field class - visit to Croatian historical Institute and the Croatian National theatre - institutions of the XIXth century  
>  11. Croatia in XIX th century  
>  12. Modern day Croatia  
>  13. students present their research  
>  14. students present their research  
>  15. Concluding remarks  
>    
>  It is mandatory to attend lectures as well as field class. Each student must present a short lecture about a specific topic of his choice.
