#### Biography
Born in 1986 in Zagreb. Graduated in 2010 from the Centre for Croatian Studies, the University of Zagreb, with majors in communication sciences. The same year she enrolled the Postgraduate doctoral study of information and communication sciences at the Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences in Zagreb. She got the title of assistant in 2011 at the Centre for Croatian Studies. She teaches the following courses: Journalism ethics, Sociological aspects of mass communication, Media pedagogy, Media and children, and Media and violence. She has participated in numerous scientific conferences in Croatia and published several scientific and professional papers in Croatian journals. Her research interests include children and the media, the media and violence, legal and ethical aspects of the mass media.
