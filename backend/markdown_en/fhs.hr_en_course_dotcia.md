**Description:**  
> - to acquaint students with basic demographic indicators and processes of development of the population of the Croatian island area  
>  - explain students the modern demographic picture of the Croatian island area  
>  - to develop students' knowledge of the peculiarities of the development of the island population  
>  - to acquaint students with the causes and consequences of depopulation processes on Croatian islands  
>  - explain the components and peculiarities of the identity of the Croatian island space  
>  - explain students the developmental approaches to the management of Croatian island space  
>    
>  The subject contributes to:  
>  - development of critical spatial thinking and understanding of specific demographic processes in Croatia  
>  - training students to understand the causes and consequences of depopulation processes in Croatia on the example of island groups  
>  - understanding the importance of Croatian island space in the context of civilization, culture and identity  
>  - thinking about demographic development concepts and approaches to managing certain parts of Croatia  
>  - training students to think critically about revitalization measures, goals and instruments in accordance with the demographic, historical and social development of the Croatian (island) area  
>  - development and improvement of general demographic, analytical and graphic skills  
>    
>  After passing the course, students will be able to:  
>  - define the term "islandness" and explain the basic demographic processes on Croatian islands  
>  - explain the basic social, geographical and demographic conditions of the modern demographic picture of the Croatian island area  
>  - define and explain the basic development problems of the Croatian island space  
>  - interpret different approaches to the management of Croatian island space on the example of selected Croatian island groups  
>  - explain the importance and manner of managing protected parts of the Croatian island area  
>  - define the degree of demographic, spatial and developmental disparity of individual islands in accordance with the geographical position and relationship to the mainland  
>  - apply relevant statistical and graphical methods in the process of reviewing and clarifying demographic patterns on islands  
>    
>    
>  1. Introduction to the subject and student obligations. Introductory considerations on the relationship between the coastal area and islands in Croatia.  
>  2. Understanding the concept of "island" in Croatia and the European Union.  
>  3. Socio-demographic classification of the Croatian island area.  
>  4. Spatial distribution and demographic differences of the Croatian island area.  
>  5. Causes and consequences of depopulation processes on Croatian islands.  
>  6. Factors of emigration from Croatian islands - historical, demographic and social overview.  
>  7. Components of the identity of the Croatian island area.  
>  8. Contemporary demographic characteristics of the Kvarner-Lošinj archipelago.  
>  9. Contemporary demographic characteristics of the North Dalmatian archipelago.  
>  10. Contemporary demographic characteristics of the Central Dalmatian archipelago.  
>  11. Contemporary demographic characteristics of the South Dalmatian archipelago.  
>  12. Development concepts of management of Croatian island area.  
>  13. Development concepts of management of Croatian protected parts of island area.  
>  14. Approaches to demographic revitalization of the Croatian island area - selected examples.  
>  15. Determinants of Croatian island development and sustainability in strategic documents. Concluding remarks.
